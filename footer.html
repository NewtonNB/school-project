STUDENT'S CLUBS


ICT Club

  • To provide a platform for ICT students to share, innovate, and enable world-standard research and learning in ICT.
  • A vibrant club where learners find a sense of belonging and actively participate in activities that enhance ICT and Computer Studies.

Interact Club

  • A club for young people who want to join and tackle issues in their community together while having fun.
  • Develop leadership skills, make international connections, help others, and carry out hands-on service projects.

Pathfinder Club

  • To introduce young people to Jesus Christ and help them develop their bodies, minds, and spirits.
  • Encourages leadership, teamwork, and holistic development physically, socially, intellectually, and spiritually.

Wildlife Club

  • Helping the young generation to enjoy and appreciate the natural world.
  • Creates awareness on balancing human activities with wildlife and teaches conservation and wildlife management.

Environmental Club

  • Educates students on steps to improve the environment and motivates them to keep surroundings green and clean.
  • Increases awareness of the relationship between human actions and the environment.

Patriotic Club

  • Helping learners to know the background of their country.
  • To love their country, protect and defend it from any evil attack.
  • Teachers and learners get committed to national services.

Writer's Club

  • Helps students to learn and practice English.
  • Enhances the knowledge of reading and writing.
  • Helps build a writing culture for the school.

Debating Club

  • Provides more information about global affairs.
  • Helps students to learn and practice English.
  • Provides training grounds for developing students' communicative skills.
  • Facilitates interactions with both local and international schools through debating competitions.

About Us

We aim to nurture confident, compassionate, and innovative leaders through inclusive education, fostering lifelong learning, critical thinking, and global awareness to inspire meaningful contributions to society and the world.
