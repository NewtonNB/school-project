Welcome to Nyabikoni Secondary School

Nyabikoni Secondary School is a private, day and boarding school for O' and A' levels. It was founded in 2003 by Rev. Fr. Busingye Christopher, a diocesan priest in Kabale Diocese.

The school is located in Kanyakiriro Cell, Nyabikoni Ward, Central Division, Kabale Municipality; a quarter kilometre along the Kabale-Kisoro main road and approximately 1 km from the municipal centre.

The idea to start the school was born out of the need to help less fortunate children in the region access education. It is registered with the Ministry of Education and Sports under registration number Pass/N/169 and UNEB Centre No. 1998.
