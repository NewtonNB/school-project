ADMISSIONS


We push ourselves to deliver the highest quality performance we do.
At Nyabikoni Secondary school we believe that those who strive for excellence will have longevity in their endeavors.

Admission Requirements

  • PLE Results / Recommendation for S.1 (New Students)
  • U.C.E Results / Recommendation for S.5 (U.C.E Students)
  • Report Card from Previous School for S.2 & S.3 (Continuing Students)
  • A Signed Copy of School Rules and Regulations Students MUST read and agree before signing;
  • A Signed Student's Bio Data Form.
  • DOWNLOADAdmission Form.

* Ensure all documents are signed where required and up-to-date.

School Requirements

Requirements

General Requirements: Each learner is required to report with the following:

  • 2 reams of paper (Rwenzori)
  • 6 toilet papers for boarders and 3 for day students
  • A folder file
  • A hard brush
  • A melamine plate and a spoon
  • At least 2 face masks

Boarders must also bring the following:

  • Mattress
  • Bed sheets
  • Towel
  • Blanket
  • Basin/bucket
  • Sponge

Scholastic Materials: Students are advised to bring enough materials to facilitate their academic endeavors. These include but are not limited to the following:

  • At least TWO dozens of black counter books and graph books for each student
  • A lab dissection kit, lab coat & gloves for students offering biology at A'level
  • A mathematical set, scientific calculator, pens, long ruler, Art books and coloured pencils
  • A ream of white ruled papers for personal use
  • Text books and pamphlets
  • Et cetera

Spiritual Requirements: Every student is required to have the following:

  • A Bible & a Hymn book
  • Students affiliated to Islam should also bring a Quran

As a student, you will be required to maintain discipline at all times.
