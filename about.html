Our Story

Founded in 1995, MediCare Hospital began with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to our community. What started as a small clinic has grown into a state-of-the-art medical facility serving thousands of patients annually.

Today, we continue to expand our services and facilities while maintaining our commitment to excellence in patient care, medical education, and research.
